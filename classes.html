Yoga Classes

Gentle Hatha Yoga
A 60 minute class of poses and slow movements that focus on asana (proper aligment and posture), pranayama (breath work), and guided medation to foster your mind and body connection, This class is intended for beginners and anyone wishing a grounded foundation in the practice of yoga.
Restorative Yoga
A 90 minute class that features very slow movement and long poses. Resorative yoga is useful in relieving stress and fostering a sense of well-being. This calming, restorative experience is suitable for students of any level of experience.
